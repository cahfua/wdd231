Why Samoa Dog Care & Adoption exists

In many Samoan villages, dogs roam freely, protect homes, and live close to families—but they don’t always get the food, shelter, or medical care they need. This project was created to bring together simple education and a sample adoption list in one place.

  • Teach families how to care for village dogs with what they already have.
  • Show clear information about dogs ready for new homes.
  • Make it easier to take a first step—learning, visiting, or adopting.
